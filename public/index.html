Mission

Our mission at San Ramon Tamil School is to foster a love for the Tamil language and culture among children and families in our community. We strive to provide a nurturing environment where students can learn to read, write, and speak Tamil fluently, while also gaining an appreciation for Tamil heritage, literature, and values. Our vision is to empower the next generation to stay connected to their roots and contribute positively to a multicultural society.
