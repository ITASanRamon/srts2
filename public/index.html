Welcome to San Ramon Tamil School (SRTS)

SRTS is dedicated to teaching the Tamil language and promoting Tamil culture to students of all ages and proficiency levelsâ€”from beginners to advanced learners. Our comprehensive program emphasizes fluency in reading, writing, and speaking Tamil, while also fostering a deep appreciation for Tamil literature, history, and traditions.
